## 1) Biotech: catalyst-rich, event-driven, and still binary

Core theme:
- biotech alpha in 2026 is being driven by regulatory acceleration, clinical readouts, and narrow windows of sentiment expansion
- psychedelic names remain the cleanest beta expression, but the broader biotech bucket now includes rare-disease and label-expansion catalysts that may be cleaner to trade

Higher-alpha names to keep active:
- CMPS: clearest pure-play psychedelic catalyst profile; strongest institutional story in the basket
- GHRS: focused depression / mebufotenin story with a clean late-stage catalyst path
- MNMD: pivotal trial readout candidate; good momentum if the data validates prior phase 2b work
- DFTX: anxiety-oriented catalyst name; useful when the market rotates from psilocybin into the broader psychedelics cohort
- VRDN: rare-disease / thyroid-eye-disease catalyst name with a defined 2026 regulatory date
- ATRA: BLA / EBV-related catalyst profile that can rerate hard on approval-path clarity
- DNL: FDA-extension / launch-prep name that can move on review updates

Signal interpretation:
- CMPS, GHRS, MNMD, and DFTX are the main pure high-beta event names
- VRDN, ATRA, and DNL are better as catalyst-specific secondary sleeves
- biotech should stay smaller-sized than photonics until there is a confirmed momentum regime

## 3) Photonics: strongest structural alpha inside the current watchlist

Core theme:
- photonics remains the cleanest AI-infrastructure theme because it is tied to the physical limits of copper, data-center bandwidth, and optical interconnect demand
- the sector is broadening from the headline names into more speculative optics and networking names

Higher-alpha names to keep active:
- AAOI: highest-beta name in the optics complex; strongest momentum profile but also the most execution-sensitive
- LITE: premium laser / transceiver leader and one of the cleanest AI-optics beneficiaries
- COHR: broader, more diversified optics anchor with strong AI infrastructure exposure
- FN: a cleaner execution name with meaningful optical supply-chain leverage
- MTSI: useful second-order photonics / semiconductor component name
- CIEN: optical systems and networking exposure for a broader infrastructure read-through

Signal interpretation:
- AAOI is the highest-alpha photonics name but should be treated carefully because volatility is extreme
- LITE and COHR are the best quality anchors
- FN and MTSI are better for steadier exposure to the same trade
- photonics should be considered the strongest structural sector among the three for the current paper session
