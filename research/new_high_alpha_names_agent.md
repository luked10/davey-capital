# New High-Alpha Names Agent

Date: Wednesday, May 13, 2026
Scope: fresh names outside the current biotech / quantum / photonics watchlist

## Research summary

The strongest new opportunities are clustering around AI infrastructure, nuclear power, space, and defense autonomy.
These names have more immediate catalyst density than the broader market and are more likely to produce paper-fillable setups.

### Highest-conviction catalyst and momentum names

1. CRDO
- AI networking / optical adjacent name with bullish options flow and pre-earnings momentum.
- Fits the AI infrastructure trade without being a direct photonics duplicate.
- One of the best near-term trigger candidates.

2. OKLO
- Advanced nuclear name tied to AI power demand.
- Recent catalysts include NRC progress, Nvidia / Los Alamos collaboration, Meta-related prepayment headlines, and continued partnership expansion.
- Very strong narrative density.

3. NBIS
- AI cloud / gigawatt-scale factory story.
- Fresh catalyst: groundbreak on the Independence, Missouri AI factory campus.
- NVIDIA partnership and large capacity buildout make this one of the cleanest new AI infra setups.

4. AVAV
- Defense autonomy / loitering munitions name with fresh Army prototype agreement and contract wins.
- Strong order-flow and headline-response potential.
- Good momentum candidate when defense is bid.

5. RKLB
- Space systems / launch momentum name with ongoing institutional attention.
- Still one of the cleanest public space comp names.
- Can trigger quickly on any launch, contract, or sector rotation news.

### Secondary but still watchable

6. ASTS
- High-volatility satellite broadband story.
- Commercialization and constellation-scale milestones keep it in play.
- More volatile than RKLB, but useful when space momentum broadens.

7. CLS
- AI data-center hardware and switching story.
- Raised 2026 outlook, sovereign AI partnership headlines, and 1.6TbE switch availability are the key drivers.
- Better execution quality than the pure speculative names.

8. VRT
- AI data-center power and cooling leader.
- Manufacturing expansion, NVIDIA alignment, and liquid cooling / 800V architecture positioning keep it well supported.
- Strong industrial AI infrastructure exposure.

9. GEV
- AI power / grid infrastructure plus nuclear and natural-gas exposure.
- Recent golden-cross / technical momentum and Blue Energy partnership headlines make it a useful power-sector comp.
