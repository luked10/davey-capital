## Signal Agent

Highest-quality near-term catalyst names:
- CMPS for psilocybin / phase 3 and FDA-path visibility
- GHRS for mebufotenin-based depression work and late-stage readouts
- DFTX for generalized anxiety phase 3 progress
- ATAI for broader psychedelics exposure, including ibogaine-related optionality

Best pure ibogaine exposure is still very small-cap and speculative:
- IBO.V / IBOGF

Peptide exposure is more durable and less policy-dependent than psychedelics:
- LLY and NVO are the quality anchors
- VKTX and PTGX are the more catalyst-driven public biotech names

## Risk Agent

Main risk buckets:
- Psychedelic drugs still face high regulatory friction, psychotherapy-integration requirements, and weak historical commercialization precedent
- Ibogaine carries meaningful cardiac safety risk and rescheduling complexity
- Most psychedelic names are cash-burn biotechs with binary trial risk
- Pure-play peptide names are usually more mature, but valuation and competitive intensity can still be heavy

Sizing note:
- Do not size psychedelic names like mature pharma
- Separate the long-duration peptide franchises from the high-volatility psychedelic names

## Current regulatory and catalyst landscape

Important current items:
- On April 24, 2026, the FDA said it is accelerating action on treatments for serious mental illness and supporting psychedelic-related development, including serotonin-2A agonists and related products
- That same FDA announcement said it allowed an early-phase clinical study of noribogaine hydrochloride to move forward after an IND submission; this was described as the first U.S. clinical study of an ibogaine derivative
- A 2023 FDA draft guidance still frames clinical investigation expectations for psychedelic drugs
- The big near-term catalysts for public psychedelic names are trial readouts, FDA designations, and any follow-on rescheduling or regulatory guidance changes

Ibogaine-specific read-through:
- Ibogaine itself remains a tightly controlled substance in the U.S.
- The public investable angle is therefore mostly through derivative programs, early clinical work, or tiny public shells rather than mature commercial assets
